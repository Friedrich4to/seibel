John P. Seibel is one of the leading Sports Law attorneys in the Dominican Republic. Throughout his years of practice, he has acquired extensive experience, primarily focused on litigation, covering civil and commercial law, criminal law, labor law, land disputes, administrative law, and alternative dispute resolution mechanisms.

His practice also encompasses advising companies on business law, with an emphasis on labor and social security matters.

His cross-cultural background, combined with his professional competence, places him in a privileged position to serve the needs of both local and foreign clients within the framework of Dominican law. This diversity of experience allows him to provide his clients with comprehensive counsel, delivering precise and effective legal solutions.
