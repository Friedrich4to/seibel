We assist companies and business groups in the design and execution of complex corporate structures, as well as in the management of large-scale transactions and cross-border operations. Our transactions and corporate practice is supported by decades of experience advising on strategic decisions that define the structure, growth, and long-term outlook of local and international organizations.

Our experience in this area includes, among others:

- Corporate operations with multiple stakeholders.
- Reorganization and business transformation processes.
- Structuring of local and cross-border investments.
- Advisory to governing bodies on strategic decisions.
- Ongoing corporate support in regulated sectors.
